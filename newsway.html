Newsway

Newsway News Summary is a real-time news summary system utilizing
the Google Gemini API to analyze Python RSS parsing as siphoned through a pipeline script to summarize breaking news every ten minutes at only the cost of the compute to run.

Utilizing Gemini’s innate Sentiment Analysis combined with a backend prompt,
I can assign optimism scores to article summaries and, as a result,
articles can be sorted by their optimism scores.

Even as an approximate measure, it has proved to reliably sieve out
negative events from positive events, especially with respect to the extremes.
