Nadette

Nadette Ai is a virtual assistant powered by Google Gemini, and it can be called and spoken to with natural language and can execute specific tasks such as multiple emails and texts to different people, making calendar events on both google calendar and icalendar, and the ability to hang up after speaking with the assurance that your last spoken requests are captured, something OpenAi doesn't yet do in their call feature for their latest llm's.
Made with Python, Bash, and Html for the email formatting.
