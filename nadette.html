Nadette

Nadette is a phone-based personal assistant you can call to execute tasks using natural voice commands.

Nadette is a phone-based personal assistant you can call and control using natural voice commands. It handles tasks like sending emails and messages, all secured with phone number whitelisting and keypad authentication. Built with Twilio, Google Gemini AI, and ElevenLabs TTS, Nadette brings hands-free productivity to your phone.
