TrueAutoColor

TrueAutoColor automatically colors your Ableton Live tracks and clips based on their names—no plugins required.

TrueAutoColor is a utility for Ableton Live 11+ that automatically colors your tracks and clips based on their names—no plugins required. Create custom color layouts with up to 70 unique Ableton colors, and trigger changes just by typing a name and pressing enter. It runs externally with minimal CPU usage, giving you a cleaner, more organized session instantly.
