GifSig

GifSig High Fiedlity Loop-Once Signature Generator is just that.
It is a faster way for someone to paste their actual dynamically drawn signature into an email, with the assurance that it only will play one time and freeze-frame at the end.
This site was fun to make but I also enjoy scribbling on it...

Because the brush implementation is responsive to drawing speed, making realistic looking strokes actually feels natural. This was made with javascript, and Vercel for the hosting and database, and resend for the account email communications and password resets.
